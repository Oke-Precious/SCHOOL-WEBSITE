2023-2024 K-12 School Placement Information

Families can complete this form to receive a student’s placement information for the 2023-2024 school year via email. You will not be able to successfully submit the form if the student's date of birth and parent/guardian email address does not match the student’s NOLA-PS record.

If you're having trouble with the form, you can request your student’s placement information through the help desk: select K-12 Enrollment / Update Student Information. Please allow up to 2 business days for an email response.

You will also receive an error message if you still need a placement for the 23-24 school year. If you need a placement or would like to choose a different school for your student, you can complete the Open Enrollment Form through September 15. For more information on Open Enrollment, visit us online.
